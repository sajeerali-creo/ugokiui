Designing Better Interfaces, Faster

We created this platform with one simple goal: to help designers build beautiful user experiences without spending hours recreating animations from scratch.

Our library provides free, ready-to-use animated UI components that can be instantly opened in Figma and integrated into any design workflow. Whether you're designing a mobile app, web application, SaaS product, or prototype, our components help you move from idea to execution faster.

What We Do

We curate and create high-quality animated UI components that are:

  • Free to use
  • Easy to customize
  • Built for modern design systems
  • Optimized for Figma workflows
  • Designed to speed up product design and prototyping

Every component is crafted to help designers, developers, startups, and product teams create polished interfaces with less effort.

Why We Built This

Designers often spend valuable time rebuilding the same interactions and micro-animations across projects. We wanted to eliminate that repetitive work by providing a library of production-ready animated components that can be used immediately.

Our mission is to make great UI design more accessible, efficient, and enjoyable for everyone—from students and freelancers to startups and enterprise teams.

Our Mission

To empower designers with free, high-quality animated components that accelerate creativity, improve consistency, and help bring ideas to life faster.
